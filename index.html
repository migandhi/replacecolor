üéöÔ∏è Step 4: Refine with Tolerance

This is the most critical step for a clean result. As soon as you pick an original color, a live preview of the replacement appears.

  • A low tolerance (e.g., 5-10) only replaces pixels that are an almost identical match.
  • A high tolerance (e.g., 50+) replaces a much wider range of similar shades and tones.
  • Adjust the slider until the live preview on your image looks perfect.

  • You can create multiple selections. Use the "Clear All" button to remove them.

üíæ Step 6: Apply and Finalize

This is where you confirm your changes and export your image.

  • Replacement Mode:

  • Apply & Save Step: Click this to "lock in" the current preview. This is necessary before you can download or undo.
  • Undo: Reverts the last "applied" step.
  • Download: Saves the final image to your device as a PNG file.
